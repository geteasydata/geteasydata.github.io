Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a hassle-free refund policy because we're confident you'll love Easy Data.

1. Our Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid subscriptions. If you're not completely satisfied with Easy Data, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase - no questions asked.

2. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • Your refund request is made within 14 days of your initial purchase
  • This is your first refund request for Easy Data
  • You have not violated our Terms of Service

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, simply:

  1. Send an email to support@geteasydata.com
  2. Include your account email address
  3. Briefly explain why you're requesting a refund (optional but appreciated)

We will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

4. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Go to your Account Settings
  • Click on "Manage Subscription"
  • Select "Cancel Subscription"

After cancellation, you will continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

5. Partial Refunds

After the 14-day guarantee period:

  • Monthly subscriptions: No partial refunds, but you keep access until the end of your billing period
  • Annual subscriptions: We may offer prorated refunds on a case-by-case basis

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Refund requests made after the 14-day guarantee period
  • Accounts terminated due to Terms of Service violations
  • Previously refunded subscriptions

7. Payment Processor

All payments are processed by Paddle, our Merchant of Record. Refunds are subject to Paddle's processing times and policies.

For payment-related issues, you may also contact Paddle directly, but we recommend reaching out to us first at support@geteasydata.com.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy:

  • Email: support@geteasydata.com
  • Website: geteasydata.github.io
